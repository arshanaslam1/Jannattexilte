Company Profile

The foundation of “Jannat Textile“ arranged with Stitching Unit 03 km Sheikhupura Road Khurianwala, Faisalabad - Pakistan. Later on Jannat Textile started export gradually. At the moment company is the suppliers of Home Textiles & Made-Ups from Pakistan and working with Directly Chain Stores and Manufacturers of Russia, Ukraine, Europe, Africa, Australia, New Zealand and America. They engage to get opportunities from different buyer all the time that they get and make these opportunities into strength with outstanding commitment all the time.

Jannat Textile has been successfully assisting suppliers and retailers connect on a global scale with the help of its robust supply chain & worldwide network.
